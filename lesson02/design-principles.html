Visual Hierarchy

Grip6

Visit Website

Grip6 is a perfect example of visual hierarchy as they display the deals on the top, then the specific store options below, and then the reviews. That helps users understand what they have on sale, what they do sell, and what people say about them!

PARC: Repetition

Easypost

Visit Website

EasyPost is the perfect example of the PARC: Repetition principle as they keep the specific styling across their entire website. This gives a consistent feel across their entire page!

Fitt's Law

YouTube

Visit Website

Youtube is the perfect example of Fitt's Law. As you enter into the website, you can see that all the important/key features are easily and quickly accessible making this website always a quick away to your destination.
