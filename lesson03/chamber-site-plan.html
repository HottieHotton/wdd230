Chamber Site Plan

By: Braxton Hotton

Site Name/Site Purpose

The Site Name will be the:
West Jordan Commerce

Our Purpose here is to serve as the online hub for the West Jordan Chamber of Commerce, providing valuable resources and information for local businesses, residents, and visitors. Our site aims to promote economic development, foster networking among businesses, and enhance the overall business climate in West Jordan. We feature business directories, event calendars, news and updates, as well as tools and services to support the growth and success of the local business community. Additionally, the site serves as a platform for collaboration, advocacy, and communication between the Chamber of Commerce and its members.
